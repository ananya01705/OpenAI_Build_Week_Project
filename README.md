# 🚀 PATHFINDER AI

> ### Predict • Simulate • Analyze • Improve
>
> **Explore Tomorrow Before You Choose It.**

PATHFINDER AI is an AI Decision Intelligence Platform that helps students make better academic and career decisions by predicting multiple future trajectories based on their current profile, goals, skills, and achievements.

Instead of simply recommending careers, PATHFINDER AI helps students answer questions like:

- Where will I be after 6 months?
- What happens if I improve my skills today?
- How do my present decisions affect my future opportunities?
- What can I do to maximize my probability of success?

PATHFINDER AI transforms student data into actionable insights by combining future simulations, success probability analysis, personalized roadmaps, and AI-powered decision intelligence.

---

## Problem Statement

Students make thousands of decisions throughout their academic journey but rarely understand how today's choices affect tomorrow's opportunities.

Questions such as:

- Which skills should I learn?
- Should I focus on projects or internships?
- How much can improving my CGPA affect my future?
- What is my probability of achieving my goals?

remain difficult to answer.

PATHFINDER AI addresses this problem by helping students visualize multiple possible futures before making important decisions.
